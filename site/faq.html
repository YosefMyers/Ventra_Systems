FAQ

What MERV should I use?

MERV 8–11 is typical for most homes. MERV 13 captures more, but can increase resistance depending on system and filter depth.

When should I replace my filter?

Commonly every 1–3 months depending on dust load, pets, occupancy, and system runtime.

Do carbon filters remove odors?

Yes—activated carbon can adsorb odors/VOCs. Capacity and dwell time matter; heavier carbon generally performs better.
